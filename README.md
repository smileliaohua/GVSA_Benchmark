# GVSA_Benchmark
Can AI Observe Geographical Space? Evaluating Geo-Visuospatial Ability of Large Multimodal Models

This “Test_results_20251209.xlsx” file contains the performance records of eight state-of-the-art multimodal large language models (MLLMs): Hunyuan-Vision, QWEN-VL-Plus, GLM-4V-Plus, Gemini-2.0, Claude-3.5-Sonnet, GPT-4o and DeepSeek-VL2, under zero-shot, zero-CoT, and one-shot prompting strategies; GPT-5 under zero-shot and complex COT.

	Hunyuan-Vision
	QWEN-VL-Plus
	GLM-4V-Plus
	Gemini-2.0
	Claude-3.5-Sonnet
	DeepSeek-VL2
	GPT-4o
	GPT-5

2.	Category:

	G-ES: Geographic Extrinsic-static (ES)
	G-ED: Geographic Extrinsic-dynamic (ED)
	NG-IS: Non-geographic Intrinsic-static (IS)
	NG-ID: Non-geographic Intrinsic-dynamic (ID)
	NG-ES: Non-geographic Extrinsic-static (ES)
	NG-ED: Non-geographic Extrinsic-dynamic (ED)

	zero shot
	zero CoT：simple COT and complex COT
	one shot

4.	Tests
	Map Overlay (Robert Bednarz & Lee, 2012)
	Spatial Positioning and Orientation(Robert Bednarz & Lee, 2012; Newcombe et al., 2015)
	Intersection Decision 
	Spatial Relation (Robert Bednarz & Lee, 2012; Jiang, 2018)
	Best Location Selection (Robert Bednarz & Lee, 2012; Jiang, 2018)
	Spatial Localization 
	Water Level (Brase & Hill, 2017)
	Spatial Construction (Robert Bednarz & Lee, 2012; Jiang, 2018)
	Spatial Deconstruction (Robert Bednarz & Lee, 2012; Jiang, 2018)
	Cube Comparison (CP) (Ekstrom et al., 1976)

	Cross Section Test (CST) (Cohen & Hegarty, 2012)
	Mental Rotation Test (MRT) (Vandenberg & Kuse, 1978)
	Purdue Spatial Visualization Test-Rotation (PD-R) (Bodner & Guay, 1997)
	Purdue Spatial Visualization Test-Development (PD-D) (Bodner & Guay, 1997)
	Purdue Spatial Visualization Test-View (PD-V) (Bodner & Guay, 1997)
	Paper Folding Test (PFT) (Ekstrom et al., 1976)
	Interpreting Contour Maps (Newcombe et al., 2015)
	Comprehending Geographic Feature (Robert Bednarz & Lee, 2012)
	Hidden Figures Test (HFT) (Ekstrom et al., 1976)
	Hidden Patterns Test (HPT) (Ekstrom et al., 1976)
	Perspective Taking/Spatial Orientation Test (PTSOT) (Hegarty, 2004; Friedman et al., 2020)
Note: Items whose question number ends with the letter “A” were generated with AI assistance. “CGFA” in Comprehending Geographic Features, “SDA” in Spatial Deconstruction, “LCA” in Spatial Localization, and “IDA” in Intersection Decision.
5.	Language
Each item was tested in Chinese and English.

6.	Test rounds
Each item was tested and re-tested under zero shot (two rounds).
